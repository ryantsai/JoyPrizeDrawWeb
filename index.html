The Mac experience follows the same seven-part story.

This page uses the Mac App Store introduction flow as its backbone: create the event, choose the animation, personalize the stage, manage entries, reveal the winner, review history, and share the draw live.

01 Create Your Own

Create your own prize and surprise draw events.

From office raffles to classroom rewards, Joy Prize Draw gives you a polished starting point for live picks on Mac. Load your entries, frame the event, and make the draw itself feel like part of the show.

  • Ideal for events, streams, workshops, classrooms, and parties.
  • Built to feel presentable on a projector, monitor, or external display.

02 Pick the Animation

Choose from eight animation modes.

Match the energy of the moment with wheel, slots, gacha, lottery, blindbox, scratch, ladder, or easter egg. Each draw can feel playful, suspenseful, or fast-moving without changing your prize data.

  • Swap styles to fit different audiences and event moods.
  • Keep the same prize pool while changing the presentation style.

03 Make It Yours

Set custom backgrounds and themes.

Personalize the stage with your own images, colors, and vibe. Whether it is a party theme, a school event, or a branded promo, the app can look like it belongs to your moment instead of fighting it.

  • Use your own visuals to create a more event-specific atmosphere.
  • Keep the interface playful while still feeling polished and intentional.

04 Set Up Fast

Edit lists in seconds.

Prize and participant setup is built for speed. Paste from a spreadsheet, bulk-load entries, add counts, and move from planning to live draw mode without wrestling with complex setup steps.

  • Great for repeat events where entry lists change every time.
  • Useful when you need to prepare a session quickly before people arrive.
